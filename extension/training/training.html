🎓 Phishing Awareness Alert
🎭
Impersonation
Phishing attacks often pretend to be trusted organizations like banks, delivery services, or government portals.
⚠️
Urgency & Fear Tactics
Messages may pressure users with threats like account suspension or limited-time actions.
🔗
Malicious Links
Phishing links redirect users to fake login pages designed to steal credentials.
🧾
Requests for Sensitive Data
Legitimate organizations never ask for passwords, OTPs, or banking details via links.
🛡️
Security Engine Detection
This content is shown because multiple detection techniques identified phishing behavior.
💡 Stay Safe:
Always verify the sender, check the website carefully, and avoid clicking unknown links. When in doubt, close the page.
